Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Summary: Zen Chime does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Your meditation sessions stay on your device.

Data Collection

Zen Chime is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect any personal information, usage data, or analytics. The app functions entirely offline.

Local Data Storage

Your meditation session history is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not accessible to us or any third parties
  • Is deleted when you uninstall the app

Third-Party Services

Zen Chime does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking services.

Permissions

The app uses the following device capabilities:

  • Audio: To play meditation bells and interval chimes
  • Background Audio: To continue playing sounds when the screen is locked

Children's Privacy

Zen Chime does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Why don't I hear any sounds?
A: Make sure your device is not in Silent Mode (check the physical switch on the side of your iPhone). Also check the volume levels in Settings.

Q: Do sounds play when my screen is locked?
A: Yes! Zen Chime continues playing interval chimes and the end bell even when your screen is locked or you switch to another app.

Q: How do I delete my session history?
A: Swipe left on any session in the History view to delete it.

Q: Is my data backed up to iCloud?
A: Currently, session data is stored locally only and is not synced to iCloud.
